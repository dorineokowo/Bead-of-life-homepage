Who We Are

We welcome those who seek more depth

Bread of Life is an urban retreat center for people who seek to expand their spiritual life or to explore personal changes more deeply.

We offer spiritual direction, programs, and gatherings for those with a desire to discover the dimensions of a deeper life explicitly connected to "Something More." We use an adult learning model that provides context and facilitates reflection on a topic's relevance to one's own life.

We welcome those who seek more depth to join us, from those who follow a specific religious tradition or path to those newly exploring the spiritual dimension of their life.

What We Offer

Programs & Groups

Bread of Life offers a various assortment of individual classes, ongoing small groups, and long term programs that foster self-discovery and connection with others.

Spiritual Direction

Spiritual Direction is an intimate and contemplative spiritual process between an individual and a trained Spiritual Director. Together the pair explore the spiritual aspects of being human, often within the context of major life circumstances or life changes.
